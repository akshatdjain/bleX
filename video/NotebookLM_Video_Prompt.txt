# NotebookLM Prompt for "BleX Smart School" Showcase

### **How to use:**
1. Upload the 'BleX_School_Knowledge_Base.md' file to a new NotebookLM notebook.
2. Paste the following prompt into the chat box to generate your script/podcast.

---

### **The Prompt:**
> You are a premium video script writer tasked with creating a high-impact demonstration video script for "BleX Smart School" — a BLE-based attendance and child safety system. This script will be used to produce a video presented to three distinct audiences: (1) Internal Demo Team, (2) Parents, and (3) School Administrators.
> 
> **YOUR TASK:**
> Using the knowledge base document provided, create a complete video script in a premium, trustworthy, Apple-product-launch style. The script should feel polished, sophisticated, and inspiring — evoking confidence and innovation.
> 
> ---
> 
> ## **SCRIPT STRUCTURE REQUIREMENTS:**
> 
> Break the video into **timed segments** (e.g., 0:00-0:15, 0:15-0:45, etc.) with a total runtime of approximately **3-4 minutes**.
> 
> For each segment, provide:
> 
> 1. **TIMECODE** — Start and end time for the segment
> 2. **VISUAL DESCRIPTION** — Detailed scene-by-scene breakdown including:
>    - Camera movements (pan, zoom, dolly, overhead shots, close-ups)
>    - Specific animation ideas (UI popups, notification pings, glowing auras around BLE cards, split screens, dynamic map views, heat maps, room transitions, graph animations, loading bars, checkmarks, smooth transitions)
>    - On-screen graphics (bold headlines, feature callouts, statistics, icons)
>    - Color palette suggestions (modern blues, whites, accent oranges/greens for alerts and confirmations)
> 3. **VOICEOVER (VO)** — The exact narration to be spoken, written in a confident, warm, and authoritative tone
> 4. **ON-SCREEN TEXT** — Key phrases, feature names, or value propositions that appear as text overlays synchronized with the voiceover
> 5. **AUDIENCE FOCUS** — Indicate which audience(s) this segment primarily addresses: [Demo Team], [Parents], [Admins], or [All]
> 
> ---
> 
> ## **CONTENT REQUIREMENTS:**
> 
> ### **Core Features to Highlight:**
> 
> 1. **Rugged Waterproof ID Cards**
>    - BLE beacons with 4-year battery life
>    - PC/ABS blend material, IP67-rated
>    - Compact, durable, child-safe design
> 
> 2. **Entry/Exit Detection**
>    - Automated detection when child enters or leaves the school premises
>    - Real-time push notifications sent instantly to parent mobile app
>    - Timestamped logs with location
> 
> 3. **Room-Level Automated Attendance**
>    - Teacher's app captures attendance in under 10 seconds
>    - Proximity-based detection as students enter classroom
>    - Zero paperwork, zero manual roll calls
> 
> 4. **Geofencing & Restricted Zone Alerts**
>    - Virtual boundaries around sensitive or hazardous areas
>    - Instant alerts to administrators and parents if a child enters restricted zones
>    - Visual map overlays showing zones and live child location
> 
> 5. **Dual-Lock Security / Proof of Life**
>    - Prevents proxy attendance (e.g., one child carrying another's card)
>    - Options: biometric verification (fingerprint/face) or dynamic session UUIDs that refresh every entry
>    - Multi-factor assurance for child's actual presence
> 
> ---
>

> 
> #### **For Parents:**
> - **Peace of mind**: Real-time notifications when child arrives/leaves school safely
> - **Transparency**: Always know where your child is during school hours
> - **Safety**: Instant alerts if child enters a restricted or unsafe area
> - **Ease**: One app, zero friction, 24/7 access to logs
> 
> #### **For School Administrators:**
> - **Automation**: Zero-paperwork attendance, reducing admin workload by 80%+
> - **Compliance**: Accurate, timestamped records for audits and regulatory requirements
> - **Safety Management**: Proactive geofencing and alerts for restricted areas
> - **Scalability**: Works seamlessly for 50 students or 5,000+
> 
> #### **For Internal Demo Team:**
> - **Technical Robustness**: BLE 5.3, low-energy architecture, 4-year battery life
> - **Reliability**: IP67 waterproof, drop-tested, child-proof
> - **Security**: Dual-lock anti-proxy technology with biometric or UUID-based verification
> - **Infrastructure**: Cloud-synced, real-time data pipeline, sub-second latency
>
